The core issue is a **unit/denomination mismatch** in fee/amount calculations during transaction validation - amounts in one unit (wei) are used directly where another unit (unibi) is expected, causing incorrect accounting.

In NEAR Protocol, the analogous units are yoctoNEAR (1 NEAR = 10^24 yoctoNEAR) and gas. Let me search for relevant code.

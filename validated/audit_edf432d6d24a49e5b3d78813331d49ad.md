### Title
Incorrect `native_per_pubdata` Calculation Uses Floor Division While `native_per_gas` Uses Ceiling Division, Systematically Undercharging Users for Pubdata - (`basic_bootloader/src/bootloader/transaction_flow/zk/validation_impl.rs`)

---

### Summary

The `native_per_pubdata` resource accounting formula uses `wrapping_div` (floor/truncating integer division) while the analogous `native_per_gas` formula uses `div_ceil` (ceiling division). This asymmetry systematically undercharges users for pubdata costs. In the degenerate but reachable case where `pubdata_price < native_price`, `native_per_pubdata` truncates to zero, allowing any transaction to produce unlimited pubdata at zero native cost.

---

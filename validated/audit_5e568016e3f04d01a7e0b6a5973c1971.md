### Title
Staker Can Instantly Raise Commission to 100% via Unguarded `set_commission_commitment`, Stealing All Delegator Yield — (File: src/staking/staking.cairo)

---

### Summary

The `set_commission_commitment` function places no upper-bound restriction on `max_commission` below `COMMISSION_DENOMINATOR` (10 000 = 100%). Once a commitment is active, `update_commission` permits the staker to raise commission to any value ≤ `max_commission` in a single call, with no time-lock or delay. A staker who initially advertised a low commission can therefore atomically set a commitment with `max_commission = 10000` and then call `set_commission(10000)`, routing 100 % of pool rewards to themselves and leaving every existing delegator with zero yield.

            assert!(staker_pool_info.has_pool(), "{}", Error::MISSING_POOL_CONTRACT);
            let current_epoch = self.get_current_epoch();
            if let Option::Some(commission_commitment) = staker_pool_info
                .commission_commitment
                .read() {
                assert!(

                    "{}",

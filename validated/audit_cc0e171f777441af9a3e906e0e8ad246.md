### Title
Unauthorized Transfer of Precompile-Controlled Bank Assets via Missing Caller Authorization in `BankContract.transfer` - (File: x/cronos/keeper/precompiles/bank.go)

### Summary
The bank precompile's `transfer` method accepts an arbitrary `sender` address as a caller-supplied argument and executes `bankKeeper.SendCoins(ctx, from, to, ...)` without verifying that `contract.Caller()` equals `sender` or that `sender` has approved the transfer. Any EVM contract can therefore drain `evm/<contract>` bank-module tokens from any victim address that holds them, with no consent from the victim.

### Finding Description
`BankContract.Run()` in `x/cronos/keeper/precompiles/bank.go` handles three write methods: `mint`, `burn`, and `transfer`. For `mint`/`burn` the denom is `EVMDenom(contract.Caller())` and the affected address is a caller-supplied argument; for `transfer` the same denom derivation is used but both `sender` and `recipient` are caller-supplied:

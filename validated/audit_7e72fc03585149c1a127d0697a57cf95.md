### Title
`FunctionInvocation::try_from` Propagates Transaction-Level `da_gas` Unchanged to All Inner Calls, Corrupting `da_gas_consumed` in RPC Simulation/Trace Responses — (`crates/apollo_rpc_execution/src/objects.rs`)

---

### Summary

`FunctionInvocation::try_from((CallInfo, GasVector))` receives the top-level `receipt.da_gas` (the total transaction DA gas) and passes it unchanged to every inner call recursively. Every nested `FunctionInvocation.execution_resources.da_gas_consumed` in the returned trace is set to the full transaction DA gas, not the per-call DA gas. Any client consuming `starknet_simulateTransactions` or `starknet_traceTransaction` receives authoritative-looking but wrong `da_gas_consumed` values for all inner calls.

---

### Finding Description

The callers in `InvokeTransactionTrace::try_from`, `DeclareTransactionTrace::try_from`, `DeployAccountTransactionTrace::try_from`, and `L1HandlerTransactionTrace::try_from` all pass `transaction_execution_info.receipt.da_gas` as the `GasVector` argument: [1](#0-0) [2](#0-1) 

Inside `FunctionInvocation::try_from`, the same `gas_vector` is forwarded to every element of `inner_calls` without modification: [3](#0-2) 

This is recursive — each inner call's own `try_from` invocation repeats the same forwarding, so the total transaction DA gas propagates to every node in the call tree at every depth.

`vm_resources_to_execution_resources` then places the received `GasVector` directly into the `da_gas_consumed` field of `ExecutionResources`: [4](#0-3) 

There is no per-call DA gas stored in `CallInfo` — DA gas is a transaction-level aggregate computed from state diffs. The code has no per-call value to substitute, so the total transaction DA gas is stamped onto every `FunctionInvocation` node in the tree.

---

### Impact Explanation

Every inner `FunctionInvocation.execution_resources.da_gas_consumed` in a simulation or trace response equals the total transaction DA gas, regardless of what that inner call actually touched. For a transaction with nonzero DA gas and any inner calls (e.g., a simple ERC-20 transfer that calls an inner contract), every inner call's `da_gas_consumed` will show the full transaction-level `{l1_gas, l1_data_gas, l2_gas}` instead of zero or its actual share. This is the "High" impact category: **RPC simulation/tracing returns an authoritative-looking wrong value**.

Tooling that uses per-call `da_gas_consumed` for cost attribution, gas profiling, or fee estimation across call frames will produce systematically inflated and incorrect results.

---

### Likelihood Explanation

Triggered by any invoke transaction with at least one inner call submitted to `starknet_simulateTransactions` or `starknet_traceTransaction`. No special privileges are required. The condition is met by the vast majority of real-world transactions (ERC-20 transfers, DEX swaps, multicalls, etc.).

---

### Recommendation

DA gas is not tracked per `CallInfo` today. The correct fix depends on the intended semantics:

- **Option A (zero inner-call DA gas):** Pass `GasVector::ZERO` for all inner calls and only use `receipt.da_gas` for the top-level `FunctionInvocation`. DA gas is a transaction-level concept and attributing it to the root call is the most defensible approximation.
- **Option B (per-call DA gas tracking):** Add a `da_gas: GasVector` field to `CallInfo` populated during execution from per-call state-diff accounting, and use that field instead of the transaction receipt's `da_gas` when constructing each `FunctionInvocation`.

Option A is a minimal, safe fix. Option B requires blockifier changes but is semantically correct.

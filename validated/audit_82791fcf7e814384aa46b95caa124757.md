### Title
Staker Can Open Delegation Pool for Inactive Token, Temporarily Freezing Delegator Funds Without Reward Accrual — (`src/staking/staking.cairo`)

---

### Summary

`set_open_for_delegation` validates only that a token *exists* (`does_token_exist`) but never checks whether it is *active* (`is_active_token`). A staker can therefore deploy a delegation pool for a token that has been added to the registry but not yet enabled (or has been disabled). Delegators who enter such a pool have their tokens transferred into the staking contract, but the reward-calculation path unconditionally skips inactive-token pools, so those delegators earn nothing while their funds are locked for the full exit-wait window.

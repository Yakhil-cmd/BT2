### Title
Instantaneous Total Staking Power Used in Attestation Reward Calculation Enables Griefing Attack - (File: `src/staking/staking.cairo`, `src/minting_curve/minting_curve.cairo`)

---

### Summary

The `update_rewards_from_attestation_contract` function computes each staker's epoch reward share using the **instantaneous** total staking power at the moment of the call. Because `tokens_total_stake_trace` is updated immediately on every `stake`, `increase_stake`, and `add_stake_from_pool` call, an unprivileged attacker can temporarily inflate the total stake just before another staker's attestation reward is processed, permanently reducing that staker's yield for the epoch.

---

### Finding Description

**Root cause — `update_rewards_from_attestation_contract`:** [1](#0-0) 

The function calls `get_current_total_staking_power()` to obtain the denominator used in the reward share calculation: [2](#0-1) 

`get_current_total_staking_power()` delegates to `get_total_staking_power_at_epoch(epoch_id: self.get_current_epoch())`: [3](#0-2) 

The underlying storage `tokens_total_stake_trace` is written **immediately** (no K-epoch delay) whenever `add_to_total_stake` is called — for example inside `stake`, `increase_stake`, and `add_stake_from_pool`: [4](#0-3) [5](#0-4) 

The internal `_get_total_stake` helper confirms it reads the latest checkpoint, not a time-weighted value: [6](#0-5) 

**Root cause — `yearly_mint`:**

The `MintingCurve.yearly_mint()` function, called inside `calculate_current_epoch_rewards`, also reads the instantaneous total staking power: [7](#0-6) 

The formula is `M = C * sqrt(total_stake * total_supply)`. Each staker's effective reward is therefore proportional to `staker_balance / sqrt(total_stake)`. Inflating `total_stake` reduces every other staker's reward for that epoch.

---

### Impact Explanation

**Impact: Medium — Griefing with no profit motive but damage to users.**

An attacker who inflates `total_stake` just before another staker's attestation reward call causes that staker to receive permanently fewer rewards for the epoch. The "missing" yield is not redistributed — it remains as `unclaimed_rewards` in the `RewardSupplier` contract and is never attributed to the victim staker. The attacker does not profit (their own reward also decreases due to the sqrt relationship), making this a pure griefing attack.

---

### Likelihood Explanation

**Likelihood: Low-Medium.**

- The attack is only effective during the **pre-consensus (attestation-based) phase** (`is_pre_consensus()` guard).
- The attacker must hold or acquire a large STRK position to meaningfully dilute rewards.
- The attacker must time the `increase_stake` / `add_stake_from_pool` call to land in the same block or just before the victim's attestation reward transaction.
- On Starknet, transaction ordering is controlled by the sequencer, so precise front-running requires either sequencer cooperation or statistical timing. However, the attacker can also simply maintain a large stake for an extended period to continuously dilute all other stakers' rewards, requiring no precise timing.
- The exit wait window (default 1 week) imposes an opportunity cost on the attacker. [8](#0-7) 

---

### Recommendation

Replace the instantaneous `get_current_total_staking_power()` call in `update_rewards_from_attestation_contract` with a time-weighted average of the total staking power over the previous epoch. Concretely:

1. Store a snapshot of `tokens_total_stake_trace` at the start of each epoch.
2. Compute the time-weighted average total stake over the epoch duration.
3. Use this averaged value as the denominator in reward share calculations, analogous to how the K-epoch delay already protects individual staker balances. [9](#0-8) 

---

### Proof of Concept

1. **Setup:** Staker A has 100 STRK staked. Total stake = 1000 STRK. Epoch rewards = R. Staker A's expected reward = `100/1000 * R = 0.1R`.

2. **Attack:** Attacker (Staker B) calls `increase_stake` with 9000 STRK. `tokens_total_stake_trace` is updated immediately to 10000 STRK.

3. **Attestation processed:** The attestation contract calls `update_rewards_from_attestation_contract` for Staker A. `get_current_total_staking_power()` returns 10000 STRK. Staker A's reward = `100/10000 * R' ≈ 0.01R'` (where R' is slightly larger due to sqrt, but net reward is still ~10× smaller).

4. **Cleanup:** Attacker calls `unstake_intent`, removing 9000 STRK from total stake immediately. After the exit wait window, attacker calls `unstake_action` and recovers all funds.

5. **Result:** Staker A permanently receives ~10× fewer rewards for that epoch. The attacker recovers their full principal with no gain, but Staker A's lost yield is never redistributed. [10](#0-9) [7](#0-6)

        fn update_rewards_from_attestation_contract(
            ref self: ContractState, staker_address: ContractAddress,
        ) {
            // Prerequisites and asserts.
            self.general_prerequisites();
            assert!(self.is_pre_consensus(), "{}", Error::CONSENSUS_REWARDS_IS_ACTIVE);
            self.assert_caller_is_attestation_contract();
            let mut staker_info = self.internal_staker_info(:staker_address);
            assert!(staker_info.unstake_time.is_none(), "{}", Error::UNSTAKE_IN_PROGRESS);

            let reward_supplier_dispatcher = self.reward_supplier_dispatcher.read();
            // Get current epoch data.
            let (strk_epoch_rewards, btc_epoch_rewards) = reward_supplier_dispatcher
                .calculate_current_epoch_rewards();
            let (strk_total_stake, btc_total_stake) = self.get_current_total_staking_power();
            let staker_pool_info = self.staker_pool_info.entry(staker_address).as_non_mut();
            let curr_epoch = self.get_current_epoch();
            self
                ._update_rewards(
                    :staker_address,
                    strk_total_rewards: strk_epoch_rewards,
                    btc_total_rewards: btc_epoch_rewards,
                    :strk_total_stake,
                    :btc_total_stake,
                    :staker_info,
                    :staker_pool_info,
                    :reward_supplier_dispatcher,
                    :curr_epoch,
                );
        }

### Title
`FeeEstimation.gas_consumed` omits L2 gas while `overall_fee` includes it, making the per-resource fields irreconcilable with the stated total — (`File: crates/apollo_rpc_execution/src/objects.rs`)

---

### Summary

`tx_execution_output_to_fee_estimation` populates `gas_consumed` with only `gas_vector.l1_gas` (L1 gas) and `data_gas_consumed` with only `gas_vector.l1_data_gas`, while `overall_fee` is set to `receipt.fee`, which is computed by `GasVector::cost()` and includes **all three** components: L1 gas + L1 data gas + L2 gas. For every V3 (AllResources) transaction with non-zero L2 gas, the equation the RPC spec declares — `overall_fee = gas_consumed × l1_gas_price + data_gas_consumed × l1_data_gas_price` — silently

### Title
Missing Inverse Validation in `assert_reshare_keys_invariants` Allows New Participant to Inject Stale Signing Key, Causing Permanent Reshare DoS — (File: src/dkg.rs)

---

### Summary

`assert_reshare_keys_invariants` in `src/dkg.rs` validates that an old participant must supply a signing key, but omits the symmetric check: that a **new** participant (absent from `old_participants`) must **not** supply one. A malicious new participant can pass a non-`None` `old_signing_key` that clears initialization validation, then causes `do_reshare` to abort mid-execution when computing Lagrange coefficients, leaving all honest participants blocked waiting for messages that never arrive.

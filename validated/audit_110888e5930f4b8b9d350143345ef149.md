### Title
Silent EVM Call on Destroyed External CRC21 Contract Causes Permanent Loss of User Funds - (File: x/cronos/keeper/evm.go)

### Summary
`ConvertCoinFromNativeToCRC21` in `x/cronos/keeper/evm.go` calls `CallModuleCRC21` on a registered external contract without verifying that the contract still has code at call time. If the external contract has been self-destructed after registration, the EVM call silently succeeds — returning empty data with no error — causing the user's native coins to be burned or locked without minting the corresponding CRC21 tokens.

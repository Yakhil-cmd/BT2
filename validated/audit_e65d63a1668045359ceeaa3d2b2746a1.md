### Title
Unbacked ERC-20 Token Minting via Direct `ft_on_transfer` Call Without Transfer Validation - (File: engine/src/contract_methods/connector.rs)

### Summary

The `ft_on_transfer` function in Aurora Engine is the NEP-141 receiver callback that mints ERC-20 tokens when NEP-141 tokens are bridged in. It validates the caller only by checking whether the `predecessor_account_id` has a registered ERC-20 mapping (`get_erc20_from_nep141`).
